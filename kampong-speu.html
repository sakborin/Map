Cardamom Mountains & Fruit Orchards

Kampong Speu, located in southern Cambodia at the foothills of the Cardamom Mountains, is a province known for its mountainous terrain, fruit orchards, and scenic landscapes. Known as "The Mountain Gateway," this diverse region offers visitors a perfect blend of highland adventures, agricultural richness, and natural beauty. Explore the foothills of the mighty Cardamom Mountains with their pristine forests and diverse wildlife, visit traditional fruit orchards famous for their mangoes and rambutans, or trek through scenic valleys where mountain streams flow through lush vegetation. The province's varied elevation creates ideal conditions for diverse agriculture and eco-tourism. With its mountain landscapes, abundant fruit production, and gateway access to Cambodia's largest wilderness area, Kampong Speu provides nature lovers and adventure seekers with authentic highland experiences while supporting local farming communities and conservation efforts in the Cardamom Mountains.
