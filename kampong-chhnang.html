Traditional Pottery & Floating Villages

Kampong Chhnang, located in central Cambodia along the Tonle Sap Lake, is a province known for its ancient pottery traditions, floating villages, and traditional craftsmanship. Known as "The Pottery Capital," this culturally rich region offers visitors a perfect blend of artisan heritage, lake life, and authentic rural experiences. Visit traditional pottery workshops where centuries-old techniques are passed down through generations, explore floating villages that rise and fall with the lake's seasonal waters, or witness skilled artisans creating beautiful ceramics using ancient methods. The province's clay-rich soil and lake access make it ideal for pottery production and fishing communities. With its renowned pottery traditions, vibrant floating communities, and skilled artisan culture, Kampong Chhnang provides craft enthusiasts and cultural explorers with authentic artistic experiences while supporting local potters and lake communities who preserve Cambodia's traditional handicraft heritage.
