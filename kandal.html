"Gateway to the Capital"

Oudong & Silk Islands

Kandal, surrounding the capital city of Phnom Penh, is a province rich in history and traditional crafts. Known as the "Gateway to the Capital," this central province offers visitors easy access to authentic Cambodian culture while serving as a bridge between urban and rural experiences. Explore the ancient capital of Oudong with its hilltop stupas and panoramic views, visit the famous Silk Islands where traditional weaving techniques are preserved, or discover the vibrant local markets of Takhmao. The province's proximity to both the Mekong and Tonle Sap rivers provides scenic boat trips and floating village experiences. With its blend of historical significance, traditional craftsmanship, and natural beauty, Kandal offers travelers an authentic glimpse into Cambodia's cultural heritage while providing convenient access to the country's political and economic center.
