"Cradle of Khmer Civilization"

Phnom Da & Angkor Borei

Takeo, located in southern Cambodia, is a province steeped in ancient history and archaeological significance. Known as the "Cradle of Khmer Civilization," this region contains some of the oldest temple ruins in Cambodia, predating even the famous Angkor complex by several centuries. Explore the ancient temple of Phnom Da with its pre-Angkorian architecture, discover the archaeological museum at Angkor Borei showcasing artifacts from the Funan Kingdom, or visit the traditional pottery villages where centuries-old techniques are still practiced. The province's numerous canals and waterways reflect its historical importance as a trading hub. With its profound historical significance, ancient temple ruins, and traditional rural lifestyle, Takeo offers travelers a unique opportunity to explore the very roots of Khmer civilization while experiencing authentic Cambodian culture in its most traditional form.
