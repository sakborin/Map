Koh Kong, located in southwestern Cambodia, is a province of pristine wilderness, dramatic waterfalls, and untouched natural beauty. Known as "The Wild Southwest," this remote region offers adventurous travelers access to some of Cambodia's most spectacular landscapes and diverse ecosystems. Explore the vast Cardamom Mountains with their dense rainforests and rare wildlife, marvel at the stunning Tatai Waterfall cascading over rocky cliffs, or trek through Botum Sakor National Park to discover hidden beaches and mangrove forests. The province serves as a gateway to eco-tourism adventures and conservation efforts. With its unspoiled wilderness, cascading waterfalls, and incredible biodiversity, Koh Kong provides nature enthusiasts with unparalleled opportunities for adventure while supporting important conservation initiatives that protect Cambodia's last remaining pristine ecosystems.
